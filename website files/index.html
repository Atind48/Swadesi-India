About Us

Swadesi was started and promoted by Ramya Ajay Raja in the year 2006, at Theni (Tamil Nadu, India) as a cottage industry. Ramya, with her flair for textile design and training in fine arts, transforms natural raw stock into usable and innovative products. As the name implies, we are committed to totally Indian and Gandhian way of producing eco-friendly products.

What We Do

We deal with ethnic Indian products which are totally eco-friendly, we offer jute & banana fibre products, khadi and organic cotton garments, eco yoga mats & accessories and much more.
